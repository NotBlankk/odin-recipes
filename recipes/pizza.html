Pizza

Discription

Pizza is an Italian dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients, baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce (usually tomato sauce)
  • Cheese (typically mozzarella)

Recipe

To make a simple pizza, start by preparing the dough. In a large bowl, combine 2 ½ cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1 tablespoon of sugar, and 1 tablespoon of active dry yeast. Gradually add 1 cup of warm water (around 110°F) and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Mix everything together until a dough forms, then knead for about 5-7 minutes until smooth. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rise for about 1 hour or until doubled in size. Meanwhile, preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out into a circle or rectangle, depending on your pan shape. Transfer the dough to a lightly greased baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the base, then sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese. Add your favorite toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, or onions. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. Slice and enjoy your homemade pizza!

Steps

  1. **Prepare the Dough:** - In a bowl, combine 2 ½ cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1 tablespoon of sugar, and 1 tablespoon of active dry yeast. - Add 1 cup of warm water (about 110°F) and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Stir until the dough starts to form. - Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it’s smooth and elastic. - Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  2. **Preheat the Oven:** - Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  3. **Shape the Dough:** - Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out into your desired shape (circle or rectangle). - Place the dough onto a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. **Add Sauce and Toppings:** - Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough. - Sprinkle with a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese. - Add your choice of toppings such as pepperoni, vegetables, olives, or any favorite ingredients.
  5. **Bake the Pizza:** - Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  6. **Serve:** - Remove the pizza from the oven, slice it up, and enjoy!
